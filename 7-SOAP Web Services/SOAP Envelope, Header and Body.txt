What is SOAP Web Service?
	
	Its an XML-Based definition of a communication of webservice
	it transfers SOAP XML Request/ SOAP XML Response
	where you have a SOAP-ENV that has Header and body

What is a SOAP Envelope?

	Its the wrapper of Soap Header and Body

What is SOAP Header and SOAP Body?

	SOAP Header contains authentication information
	body contains the raw information that you are
	wanting to send to the client/consumer
